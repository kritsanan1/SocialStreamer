Requirements & Features
Core Features:
Compose posts with text and media attachments.
Schedule posts for future publication.
Post to multiple social networks including Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, TikTok, X/Twitter.
View post history and status with filtering options for successful and failed posts.
Advanced Analytics Dashboard for insights into post performance.
AI-Powered Content Suggestions for optimal content and posting times.
Team Collaboration Tools with role-based access controls.
Customizable Post Templates for repeated formats.
Automated Post Rescheduling for failed posts.
Integration with Canva and Grammarly for enhanced content creation.
Enhanced Security Features including two-factor authentication.
User Flows
User signs in and links their social media accounts via the Business Plan.
User composes a post, schedules it, and selects multiple platforms for posting.
User reviews post history and filters posts by status.
User accesses advanced analytics to gauge engagement and performance.
Team members collaborate on post creation with specific roles and permissions.
Business Rules
Only users with a Business Plan can link social media accounts.
Posts can be scheduled for future dates and times, adhering to each platform's API restrictions.
Role-based access control for team collaboration.
AI suggestions are generated based on engagement data.
UI/UX Design
Layout
Responsive grid layout for desktop and mobile, ensuring optimal usability across devices.
Look & Feel
Modern and clean design with a focus on ease of use and accessibility.
Pages
Dashboard: Overview of scheduled and past posts, with analytics insights.

Analytics: Detailed insights into post performance and engagement.
Components
